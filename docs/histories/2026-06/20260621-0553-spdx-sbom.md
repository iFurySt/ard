## Design Notes

The SBOM generator is checked into the repository instead of downloaded during CI. That
keeps release packaging agent-legible, avoids a new tool bootstrap step, and gives
operators a concrete dependency inventory today. Signed provenance and release
attestations remain the next supply-chain milestone.
